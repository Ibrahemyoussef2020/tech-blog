how does the modern technology helps us in computer science

Digitalization is inexorable, and it is occurring at an unprecedented rate. IoT, robots, artificial intelligence, blockchain, and big data are examples of new technologies that have the potential to transform the way we live, work, and communicate. Without a doubt, technology will be a critical component of the majority of people's jobs in the future, with everyone, at all levels, requiring far more advanced technological capability than they do today. Data analytics, artificial intelligence and machine learning, human-centric interactions, and information security specialists will be in high demand.
